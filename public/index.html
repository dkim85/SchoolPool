Make Every Trip to College
a Smart Trip

Have you ever had trouble finding a friend to carpool with on the weekends or for the holidays? Did you ever wish that there was a way to connect with others who can either give you a ride or pitch in for some gas?
Say goodbye to your stress and say hello to School Pool! This app provides an efficient alternative to carpooling from campus to any location that you desire. Post your own ride if you are looking for others to join you on your journey or search for people who are already driving and looking for passengers.
So what are you waiting for? Start using SchoolPool and make your drives from school more enjoyable!
